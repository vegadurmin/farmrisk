FarmRisk Agro-Meteorological Advisory Platform
Project Type: Web-and-App-based Climate and Weather Advisory Platform for Agriculture
Target Users: Farmers, Agricultural Extension Officers, NGOs, Government Agencies, Agri-Tech Companies
________________


1. Executive Summary
FarmRisk is a multilingual agro-meteorological decision support platform that provides location-specific weather forecasts, climate advisories, and agricultural recommendations to farmers. The platform translates complex weather and climate information into actionable insights that help farmers make informed decisions regarding irrigation, sowing, harvesting, pest management, and risk mitigation.
The system integrates weather forecasts, lightning alerts, crop-specific advisories, and climate intelligence into a simple and mobile-friendly interface.
________________


2. Problem Statement
Current Challenges
Farmers face several climate-related uncertainties:
Weather Uncertainty
* Unpredictable rainfall patterns
* Increasing frequency of extreme weather events
* Heatwaves and droughts
* Flash floods and waterlogging
Information Accessibility
* Weather information is often difficult to understand
* Most forecast products are not localized
* Limited availability in regional languages
* Lack of actionable agricultural recommendations
Decision-Making Challenges
Farmers need answers to questions such as:
* Should I irrigate today?
* Is rainfall expected in the next few days?
* Is it safe to apply fertilizers?
* Should harvesting be postponed?
* Is there a risk of lightning?
* What precautions should be taken during heavy rainfall?
Without reliable information, farmers often incur losses due to poor timing of agricultural actions and operations.

3. Project Objectives
Primary Objectives
1. Accurate location-specific weather forecasts.
2. Convert weather forecasts into actionable farm advisories.
3. Improve climate resilience among farmers.
4. Reduce crop losses caused by weather extremes.
5. Provide information in local languages.
6. Support data-driven agricultural planning.
Secondary Objectives
* Increase adoption of climate-smart agriculture.
* Enhance preparedness against extreme events.
* Improve irrigation efficiency.
* Support government and extension services.

5. Core Features
________________


5.1 Location Selection
Purpose
Allow users to select their village (free) or farmland (SaaS).
Features
* Search village/location, GPS-based location detection, 
* Recent locations, Favorite locations
Outputs
* Administrative boundaries
* Village information
________________


5.2 Interactive Map
Purpose: Provide geographical context.
Features
* Satellite view, Terrain view, Administrative boundaries
* Lightning overlays, Weather layers
Benefits
Users can visually identify their farm area and weather conditions.
________________


5.3 Current Weather Dashboard
Parameters: Temperature, Feels Like, Humidity, Wind Speed, Pressure, Cloud Cover, Rainfall Today
User Benefits: Quick weather assessment, Planning daily activities, Monitoring heat stress conditions
________________


5.4 Hourly Forecast (24 Hours)
Parameters: Temperature, Rainfall probability, Rainfall amount, Weather condition, Wind
Use Cases: 
Irrigation Planning (Avoid irrigation before expected rainfall)
Fertilizer Application (Schedule applications during dry periods)
Harvest Planning (Avoid harvesting during rainfall events)
________________


5.5 10-Day Crop-Specific-Advisory Outlook
Purpose: Generate easy-to-understand agricultural guidance
Advisory Inputs: Weather forecast, Rainfall forecast, Temperature trends, Crop stage, Seasonal climatology
Example Recommendations
Rain Expected: Avoid irrigation, Delay pesticide spraying
Dry Conditions: Schedule irrigation, Monitor soil moisture
Heavy Rainfall: Improve field drainage, Protect harvested produce
High Temperature: Increase irrigation frequency, Protect livestock


Example Crop-Specific-Recommendations
Best sowing windows, When and how much to irrigate, Optimal fertilizer timing, Weather-driven risk alerts, Optimal harvest dates.

6. Functional Requirements
Weather Data Processing: The system shall update forecasts daily, Store historical forecasts, Validate incoming weather data, Generate forecast summaries.
________________


Advisory Engine
The system shall:
* Convert forecast data into advisories using LLM APIs, Generate crop-specific recommendations (RAG architecture), Support multilingual outputs, Prioritize critical warnings.
________________


User Interface
The system shall: Be mobile responsive, Load within 3 seconds, Support low-bandwidth environments, Work across browsers
